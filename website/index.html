How it works

Three Steps. Under 50ms.

Set your rules, call one endpoint, get a decision. Your agent never has to wait.

Step 1

Define Your Rules

Pick from 10 rule types and combine them into a policy. Set dollar limits, vendor lists, time windows, geography blocks, discount caps, or duplicate detection.

max_amount vendor_allowlist time_restriction escalate_if duplicate_guard +5 more

Step 2

Your Agent Calls One Endpoint

Before every financial action, your agent sends the details to POST /v1/checks. SpendGuard evaluates all the rules in the policy and returns a decision. Your agent barely notices the extra call.

Step 3

Get Allow, Block, or Escalate

Every response tells you exactly what happened: which rule fired, why it was blocked, and what the agent should do next. Every decision is permanently logged in the audit trail.

FAQ

Common Questions

How Does the Authorization Flow Work?

Your agent sends a check request with the action details (amount, type, counterparty). SpendGuard loads your policy, evaluates every rule, and returns allow, block, or escalate in under 50ms. The entire decision is logged permanently.

What Data Do You Store?

Only action metadata: amounts, action types, decisions, and rule outcomes. We never store bank account numbers, SSNs, credit card details, or any personally identifiable information. API keys are SHA-256 hashed and the original is never stored.

What Happens When My Agent Hits the Limit?

The API returns a 402 response with your exact usage numbers and plan limit. On paid plans, you can enable pay-per-check overage at $0.005 per check for the rest of the billing period. Free tier users need to upgrade. No surprise charges ever.

Can I Test Without Affecting Real Data?

Yes. The simulation endpoint runs the exact same rule engine but writes nothing to any table. No audit records, no usage events, no duplicate fingerprints. You can call it as many times as you want with zero impact on your data.

How Fast Is the API?

The rule engine alone runs in under 10ms. Total end-to-end latency including network, database lookup, and audit logging is typically under 50ms. Most compliance and authorization tools add 500ms or more. Your agent will not notice the extra call.

Do I Need a Credit Card to Start?

No. The free tier gives you 1,000 checks per month with just your name and email. No credit card, no trial period, no expiration. Upgrade to a paid plan whenever you need more volume.
